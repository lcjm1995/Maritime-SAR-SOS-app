Essential information - Ainsdale Beach

  • Opening times: Open all year round
  • Lifeguard cover: No
  • Parking: Beach parking off Shore Road, Ainsdale. Car park charge £4.50 - £6.00
  • Dog friendly: Yes, but there is a dog free zone for 200m either side of the beach entrance at Shore Road.
  • Toilets: Yes, at the beach entrance at the end of Shore Road.
  • Local shops: Ainsdale village has shops, cafes and restaurants
  • Ice cream: There are usually ice cream vans dotted around the area
